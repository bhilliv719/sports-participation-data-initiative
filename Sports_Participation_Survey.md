## Analysis Framework
- **Segmentation:** Grade level, current athlete vs. non-athlete
- **Barrier analysis:** Ranked most common reasons for non-participation
- **Correlation:** Athletic confidence vs. participation rate
- **Channel effectiveness:** How students hear about sports opportunities
- **Actionable outputs:** What changes would increase likelihood of joining
